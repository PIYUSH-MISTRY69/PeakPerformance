DEADLIFT

1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, bend knees slightly, and grip the barbell with an overhand or mixed grip.

2. Keep your back straight, engage your core, and lift the barbell slowly, pushing through your heels.

3. Start with light weight initially and learn correct form. Increase weight gradually.

LAT PULLDOWNS

1. Sit on a lat pulldown machine, grasp the bar with an overhand or underhand grip.

2. Pull the bar down towards your chest, keeping your elbows close to your body, chest lifted.

3. Try squeezing your lats for maximum outcome.

BENT OVER ROWS

1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, bend at the hips and knees.

2. Grasp the barbell with an overhand or underhand grip and pull it towards your belly, keeping your back straight.

3. Hold your neck at the same position throughout the set.

SEATED CABLE ROW

1. Sit on a seated cable row machine, grasp the handles with an overhand grip.

2. Pull the handles towards your belly, keeping your elbows close to your body.

3. Lean slightly forward while leaving the cable away.

SINGLE ARM ROWS

1. Hold dumbell with single arm, resting opposite knee onto the bench. Try looking down.

2. Keeping your back straight, row the dumbbell towards your waist.

3. Use the free leg for proper stability.
