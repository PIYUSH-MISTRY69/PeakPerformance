Squats

1.Feet and Posture: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, chest up, and back straight.

2. Descent: Bend knees and hips to lower down, keeping knees in line with toes.

3. Ascent: Push through heels to rise, fully extending hips and knees.

Lunges

1. Starting Position: Stand with feet hip-width apart, shoulders back.

2. Descent: Step forward with one foot, lower hips until both knees are at 90 degrees.

3.Ascent: Push through the front heel to return to starting position.

Calf Raises

1. Starting Position: Stand with feet hip-width apart and weight evenly distributed.

2. Raise: Lift heels off the ground, standing on your toes, and squeeze calves.

3. Lower: Slowly lower heels back to the ground to complete the movement.

Glute Bridges

1.Starting Position: Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor, hip-width apart.

2. Lift: Raise your hips towards the ceiling, squeezing your glutes at the top.

3. Lower: Slowly lower your hips back to the floor, maintaining control throughout.

Bulgarian Split Squats

1. Setup: Place one foot behind you on a chair or bench, with the other foot forward.

2. Lower: Bend the front knee to lower your body until the back knee nearly touches the ground.

3. Lift: Push through the front heel to return to the starting position.
