Jumping Jacks

1.Start: Stand with feet together and arms at your sides.

2. Jump: Jump while spreading your legs and raising your arms overhead.

3. Return: Jump back to the starting position and repeat.

High Knees

1. Start: Stand with feet hip-width apart.

2. Lift: Quickly alternate bringing your knees up towards your chest.

3. Pump: Pump your arms in rhythm with your legs to increase intensity.

Burpees

1. Squat: Start in a squat position with hands on the floor.

2. Jump: Jump your feet back into a plank position, then perform a push-up.

3. Rise: Jump your feet back to the squat position and explode upward into a jump.

Mountain Climbers

1. Start: Begin in a plank position with your hands under your shoulders.

2. Drive: Alternate bringing your knees towards your chest in a running motion.

3. Maintain: Keep your hips stable and core engaged throughout.

Box Jumps

1. Start: Stand in front of a sturdy, elevated surface (like a box or step).

2. Jump: Bend your knees slightly and jump onto the surface, landing softly with knees slightly bent.

3. Step Down: Step back down carefully and repeat.
