CORE

Planks

  1. Position: Get into a push-up position, keeping your body in a straight line.
  2. Engage: Tighten your core and hold the position.
  3. Maintain: Keep your hips level and avoid sagging or lifting your buttocks.

Crunches

  1. Position: Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat.
  2. Lift: Curl your upper body towards your knees, engaging your abs.
  3. Lower: Slowly return to the starting position.

Bicycle Crunches

  1. Position: Lie on your back, lift your legs to a 90-degree angle, and place hands behind your head.
  2. Twist: Alternate bringing opposite elbows to knees while extending the other leg.
  3. Switch: Continue the pedaling motion in a controlled manner.

Leg Raise

  1. Position: Lie on your back with legs straight and arms at your sides.
  2. Lift: Raise your legs towards the ceiling, keeping them straight.
  3. Lower: Slowly lower your legs back to just above the ground without touching it.

Russian Twist

  1. Position: Sit with knees bent and lean back slightly, holding your hands together.
  2. Twist: Rotate your torso to one side, touching the floor beside your hip.
  3. Switch: Twist to the opposite side, continuing the alternating motion.
