STRECHING

Hamstring

  1. Position: Sit on the floor with one leg extended and the other bent.
  2. Reach: Lean forward and reach towards the toes of the extended leg.
  3. Hold: Keep your back straight and hold the stretch, then switch legs.

Quad

  1. Position: Stand on one leg and grab your opposite ankle behind you.
  2. Pull: Gently pull your ankle towards your glutes.
  3. Hold: Keep your knees together and hold, then switch legs.

Chest

  1. Position: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Extend: Place your arms behind your back and clasp your hands.
  3. Lift: Gently lift your arms and open your chest, holding the stretch.

Shoulder

  1. Position: Stand or sit with one arm across your chest.
  2. Pull: Use the opposite arm to gently pull the extended arm closer to your chest.
  3. Hold: Keep your shoulder down and hold the stretch, then switch arms.

Cat Cow

  1. Position: Start on your hands and knees in a tabletop position.
  2. Arch: Inhale and arch your back (cow), lifting your head and tailbone.
  3. Round: Exhale and round your back (cat), tucking your chin and tailbone.
