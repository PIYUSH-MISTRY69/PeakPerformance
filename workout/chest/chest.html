Flat Dumbbell Bench Press

1.Lie on a bench, holding dumbbells with palms facing forward

2. Lower the weights to your chest, keeping your elbows slightly flared.

3. Press the weights back up, squeezing your chest at the top.

Cable Flye

1. Stand between two cable machines, facing away.

2. Grasp handles and step forward, slightly bending elbows.

3. Slowly bring handles together in front of chest, squeezing chest muscles. Return to starting position, focusing on controlled movement.

Incline Dumbbell Press

1. Lie on an incline bench, holding dumbbells at chest level.

2. Press them up until your arms are fully extended, keeping your elbows slightly inward.

3. Lower the weights back to your chest, maintaining control throughout.

Machine Flye

1. Sit on the machine, grab the handles, and keep your elbows slightly bent.

2. Inhale as you slowly open your arms out to the sides, pausing briefly at the bottom.

3. Exhale as you bring your arms back together, squeezing your chest muscles.

Wide Grip Bench Press

1. Lie on a bench, grab the barbell with a wider-than-shoulder-width grip, and lower it to your chest while keeping your elbows flared out.

2. Press the barbell back up to the starting position.

3. Keep your core engaged and your feet flat on the floor.
