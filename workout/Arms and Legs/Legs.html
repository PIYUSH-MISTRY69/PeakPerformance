LEGS

High Bar Squat

  1. Setup: Position the barbell high on your traps, feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower: Bend your knees, keeping chest up and knees aligned with toes.
  3. Rise: Drive through your heels to stand back up.

Leg Extension

  1. Setup: Sit on the leg extension machine, adjust the pad to rest just above your ankles.
  2. Extend: Straighten your legs by lifting the weight until your knees are fully extended.
  3. Lower: Slowly lower the weight back to the starting position without letting it drop.

Leg Press

  1. Setup: Sit on the leg press machine, feet shoulder-width apart on the platform, and knees at a 90-degree angle.
  2. Press: Push the platform away by extending your legs without locking your knees.
  3. Lower: Slowly bend your knees to return to the starting position while keeping control of the weight.

Lying Leg Curl

  1. Setup: Lie face down on the leg curl machine, positioning the pad just above your ankles.
  2. Curl: Flex your knees to lift the weight, bringing your heels toward your glutes.
  3. Lower: Slowly extend your legs back to the starting position.

Seated Leg Curl

  1. Setup: Sit on the leg curl machine, adjust the pad just above your ankles.
  2. Curl: Bend your knees to pull the pad down, bringing your feet toward your glutes.
  3. Release: Slowly extend your legs back to the starting position.
