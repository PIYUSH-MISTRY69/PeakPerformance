Leaning Cable Lateral Raise

1.Attach a cable handle to the lowest setting on a cable machine and lean away from it, grabbing the handle with your outside arm.

2. With a slight bend in your elbow, raise your arm out to the side until it's parallel to the ground, pausing at the top.

3. Slowly lower your arm back to the starting position, maintaining tension throughout the movement.

Dumbbell Shrug

1. Stand tall with dumbbells at your sides.

2. Raise your shoulders as high as possible, squeezing your trapezius muscles.

3. Lower your shoulders back to the starting position and repeat.

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press

1.Sit upright on a bench, holding dumbbells at shoulder height.

2. Press the dumbbells overhead until your arms are fully extended, keeping your elbows slightly bent.

3. Slowly lower the weights back to the starting position, maintaining control throughout the movement.

Lateral Raise

1. Sit on the machine, grab the handles, and keep your elbows slightly bent.

2. Inhale as you slowly open your arms out to the sides, pausing briefly at the bottom.

3. Exhale as you bring your arms back together, squeezing your chest muscles.

Standing Barbell Shoulder Press

1. Stand tall with your feet shoulder-width apart.

2. Hold dumbbells with your palms facing forward.

3. Raise your arms out to the sides until they're parallel to the ground.
