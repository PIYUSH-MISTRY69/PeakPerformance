Leaning Cable Lateral Raise

  1. Attach a cable handle to the lowest setting and lean away from it.
  2. With a slight bend in your elbow, raise your arm until it's parallel to the ground.
  3. Lower your arm back to the starting position, maintaining tension.

Dumbbell Shrug

  1. Stand tall with dumbbells at your sides.
  2. Raise your shoulders as high as possible, squeezing your trapezius muscles.
  3. Lower your shoulders back and repeat.

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press

  1. Sit upright on a bench, holding dumbbells at shoulder height.
  2. Press the dumbbells overhead until your arms are fully extended.
  3. Lower the weights back to the starting position, maintaining control.

Lateral Raise

  1. Sit on the machine, keep your elbows slightly bent.
  2. Slowly open your arms out to the sides, pausing briefly at the bottom.
  3. Exhale as you bring your arms back together, squeezing your shoulders.

Standing Barbell Shoulder Press

  1. Stand tall with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Press the barbell overhead until your arms are fully extended.
  3. Lower the barbell back down with control.
